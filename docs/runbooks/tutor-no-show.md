# Tutor no-show

Use when the tutor may have missed a scheduled demo. Silence alone is not sufficient evidence.

## Triage

1. Confirm the session state, UTC/local time, grace window, required confirmations, Calendar event,
   delivery status, tutor acknowledgement, join/attendance signals, and technical-failure reports.
2. Apply the versioned no-show policy. Distinguish tutor no-show, learner no-show, technical failure,
   provider delivery failure, and unknown evidence.
3. Protect tutor and learner contact data; coordinate through authoritative references and approved
   channels.

## Respond

- Open the audited human-handoff/SLA path and notify regional operations.
- Give the learner a truthful apology/status without assigning blame before evidence is complete.
- Offer an approved reschedule or replacement workflow; never silently book a new tutor or release a
  valid old booking before replacement is secure.
- Cancel/update Calendar and reminders only through idempotent saga operations.
- Record evidence, policy version, outcome, remedy, and any reviewed quality/performance impact.

Repeated tutor no-shows feed privacy-safe aggregates only after minimum-sample and policy review;
they do not automatically expose private performance data or make a discriminatory ranking decision.
